About CoderzHub




Welcome to CoderzHub, a site made for programmers by programmers. We are dedicated to providing a way for every professional, student, and hobbyist to link with like-minded people and do what they love to do: code.


CoderzHub was created during Fresno, California's 5th annual 59 Days of Code competition. Originally meant to be used as a tool for computer science majors to connect with each other and form online study groups, the idea evolved into a social network for all programmers. We encourage users to be inventive, ask and answer questions about code or programming langauges, and to collaborate on projects.


We invite you to connect and create!
